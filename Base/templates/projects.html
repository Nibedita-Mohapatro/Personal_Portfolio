HAL SmartMove

Year: 2025-2025

Technologies:Frontend: React,HTML,CSS
Backend: Python AI/ML
Database:SQLLite3

Role:Idea Proposal,Project Coordinator,Database design,Frontend Development to some extent

GitHub

During my internship at HAL, I contributed to the development of the SMARTMOVE project, which addressed the lack of a digital system for employee transportation. The project enables employees to submit their travel details online, allowing the transport department admin to efficiently assign the most suitable driver and vehicle. This streamlined process enhances transportation management and improves overall operational efficiency.
The system includes three user profiles: Employee, Driver, and Admin. Employees can submit transportation requests through the platform. The Admin reviews these requests, assigns the best-suited driver and vehicle, and approves the assignments. Drivers have access to functionalities such as GPS integration and real-time updates to facilitate smooth transportation operations.
The project was divided into three parts. I was primarily responsible for database management and concurrently learned React to assist with frontend development alongside my teammate. I also contributed to frontend tasks, gaining valuable experience in full-stack development.
